Case Study

Designing a new website and social media presence for FunClaw in Indonesia

Digital & Content Designer · 2023 – 2024 · Indonesia
Web Design Social Media Strategy Video Production Merchandise

FunClaw is an arcade business from Singapore that expanded to Indonesia. They needed a localized digital presence. I helped them build a new website and create social media content for the Indonesian audience.

FunClaw Indonesia
Contents
  • Overview
  • The Challenge
  • My Approach
  • Brand Assets
  • Web Design

Overview

Creating a local online presence

FunClaw operates claw machine locations. For their move to Indonesia, I was hired to design their new website and social media graphics using their existing brand guidelines.

I worked with their team to establish their online presence from the ground up, providing a complete package of web and social materials.

12Months of work
64Instagram posts
1Website built
The Challenge

The Challenge

FunClaw needed social media content and a website that appealed specifically to the Indonesian market while keeping their established visual style.

Market Needs
  • Appealing to a younger gaming audience
  • Creating a completely new social media account
  • Applying existing guidelines to new formats
  • Working with a tight timeline
Project Goals
  • Use current brand colors and logos
  • Build a functional Indonesian website
  • Design content for a new Instagram account
  • Design localized physical items
My Approach

My Approach

I created a set of reusable design components based on their original brand guidelines. This made it easier to produce consistent content for their website and social media without constantly starting from scratch.

01
Brand Adaptation
I adjusted their visual style to fit a younger demographic in Indonesia.
02
Website Design
I designed and built their Indonesian website with a focus on mobile usability.
03
Content Creation
I created templates for their Instagram posts, stories, and videos.
04
Physical Items
I applied the design to physical items like cups and stickers.
Brand Assets

Using existing brand assets

I took FunClaw's existing colors and visual elements and applied them to new templates for the Indonesian market. The goal was to keep the brand recognizable while making it fresh for a new audience.

Established brand elements applied to new digital channels.

Deliverables in this phase:

Visual ReviewDesign TemplatesMerchandiseSocial GraphicsEvent Photography
Web Design

Website Design

I built a dedicated website for Indonesia. The focus was on helping users find locations and view the games easily on their phones.

The local website designed using FunClaw's core visual system.
Social Graphics

Social Media Graphics

I designed the first batch of Instagram posts to start their new account. I also edited short videos for their social media feed to showcase the locations and games.

Feed PostsStories TemplatesReel VideosOnline AdsEvent Photos

Case Study

Building a Wix website from Figma designs

Web Designer · 2024 · Vietnam
Web Development Wix Figma

Polar Ice Cream had existing designs in Figma for their Vietnam website. They needed someone to build the site in Wix exactly as designed.

Polar Ice Cream
Contents
  • Overview
  • The Challenge
  • My Approach
  • Execution
  • Results
Overview

Accurate website development

The design team created detailed website prototypes. My task was to take those designs and build them accurately in Wix.

1:1Design accuracy
0Developers needed
FastDelivery time
The Challenge

The Challenge

The designs included specific spacing, animations, and layouts. The goal was to recreate them perfectly in Wix.

Technical Needs
  • Working with complex Figma files
  • Adding custom animations
  • Matching fonts and layouts exactly
  • Using Wix Studio
Project Goals
  • Accurate translation of designs
  • Making sure the site worked on mobile phones
  • Setting up the product catalogue
My Approach

My Approach

I reviewed the Figma files and set up the global colors and fonts in Wix. Then, I built each page section by section.

01
Review
I looked over the Figma files to understand all the required components and interactions.
02
Setup
I added the brand colors and fonts into Wix.
03
Building
I built each section to match the designs exactly.
04
Testing
I tested the website on mobile, tablet, and desktop screens.
Reviewing the Figma files to prepare for the build.
Execution

The Build Process

I spent extra time on the product gallery to make sure the hover effects worked correctly. I also set up the content manager so the team could easily add new products without changing the layout.

The final website built in Wix Studio.
